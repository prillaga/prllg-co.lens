Payment methods

Here is how payments work for Prillaga&Co. Lens: a small reservation fee to hold your dates, then the rest when you pick up the gear. After we confirm your booking in Messenger, use the official GCash number and QR below for the reservation fee, then send us proof using the Messenger button.

Reservation fee (₱100)

After you receive confirmation in Messenger, send the ₱100 reservation fee to secure your slot. First paid, first served. This amount is deducted from your total rental when you settle the balance.

GCash is the usual way guests send this fee; if you use another channel we accept, staff will confirm the instructions with you in chat.

GCash — reservation fee (₱100)

Business owner: set PRILLAGA_GCASH_NUMBER and PRILLAGA_GCASH_QR_SRC in site-config.js. Put the QR file under images/ (e.g. images/gcash-qr.jpg) or use a full URL.
