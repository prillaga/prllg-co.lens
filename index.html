Rental Policy

📸 HOW TO RENT

1. Send us a message with your preferred rental date and the camera you would like to rent.
We will check availability and confirm your booking.

2. Pay a ₱100 reservation fee to secure your slot.
✔ First to pay, first served.
✔ Payment methods (GCash, Maya, GoTyme, online banking, and more).
✔ The reservation fee will be deducted from the total rental amount.
✔ Pay your balance payment upon meet up.

3. Pick-up locations: SM City, Robinsons Main, JKS Vape Shop - North Baluarte, University Dorm, University of San Agustin

4. Sign the rental agreement (sign online here) and leave one (1) valid ID as a deposit upon meet up. Pay your balance.
✔ Your valid ID will be returned once the camera is safely returned and checked.

5. Handle the camera with care as if it were your own.
The renter is responsible for any damages, loss, or missing accessories.

📌 Additional Reminders:

• Late returns may be subject to additional charges.
• Please check the unit upon pick-up to ensure everything is complete and working properly.
• Make sure the camera is returned on time and in good condition.

Thank you for choosing us! We look forward to serving you 📷✨
